Welcome Foodies!


I am a lifelong healthy food lover and here I have gathered some of my favourite recipes. Several years ago I got interested in vegetarian food and since then I tried to focus my attention on delicious vegetarian recipes. I think it's important to have a healthy variety on your plate this is why I put my collection online to help other people who are trying to reduce the consumption of meat find their new favourite meals. I have a plenty of vegan recipes too. However, since I'm not a vegan, I often suggest some yoghurt to enrich the taste which is optional, of course.

The main reson why I'm trying to reduce my meat consumption is because of the hormones and antibiotics that most of the meat found on the market contains. In addition to that there are many theories according to which even organic meat is not the best food for the human body. Honestly, I'm not sure what you can believe because all food theories sound logical in some way. This is why I experiment and trust my own body. So far eating vegetarian food makes me feel better than my previous diet and gives me plenty of energy.

So are you ready for this healthy culinary adventure? ;)
